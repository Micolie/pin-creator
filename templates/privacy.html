Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Olu the Chef Pin Creator app, developed by Micolie, handles information when you use this application.

1. Personal Use Only

This app is for personal use only. It is not distributed to the public and is not available for download by third parties.

2. Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The only data accessed is the Pinterest account of the developer for the purpose of creating pins.

3. Pinterest API

The app uses the Pinterest API to create pins on behalf of the developer's own Pinterest account. No data is shared with third parties beyond what is required by the Pinterest platform to complete the pin creation.

4. Authentication

Pinterest OAuth tokens are stored in session memory only and are not persisted to any database or third-party service.

5. Cookies

The app uses a session cookie solely to keep you logged in during your visit. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.
